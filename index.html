Rating System

Shiftly uses a two-way rating system where both businesses and student workers can rate each other after a shift. This ensures transparency, accountability, and a better experience for future hires.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Students

How do I sign up as a student?

Sign up with your .edu email, create a profile, and start applying for shifts.

How does payment work?

Students are paid directly by the restaurant.

Do students need prior experience?

No experience required! Sign up and browse the various jobs.

Businesses

How do I sign up as a business?

Create a business account, agree to our Terms of Service, and start posting shifts.

Is there a fee for businesses?

Posting shifts is free. A 30% service fee applies only when a shift is filled.

How do you ensure the qualifications of the sutdent workers

Businesses can set requirements for certain ratings, Shiftly badges, language fluency, and more for shifts.
